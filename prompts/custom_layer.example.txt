CUSTOM LAYER (example — replace with your own instructions)

Copy this file to `custom_layer.txt`, point CUSTOM_LAYER_FILE at it in config.sh, and
flag the channels you want it applied to as `custom` in channels.tsv.

Whatever you write here is appended to the digest prompt for those channels, so the model
adds one extra section at the end. Keep any private/domain-specific logic here — it is
gitignored and never leaves your machine.
